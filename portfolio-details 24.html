Dataframes with Pandas - Students Health and Academic Performance

  • Skill: Python
  • Library: Pandas and Numpy
  • Project date: 5 July, 2024
  • Project URL: github

This dataset explores the relationship between students' health and their academic performance. It contains multiple rows of data, each representing a student, and multiple columns. This dataset can be used to analyze the impact of health on academic success, identify potential predictors of academic performance, and inform interventions to support students' overall well-being and academic achievement. We explore dataframe methods like info, describe, value_counts, sort_values, and iloc. We concatenated 2 dataframes with the same format and demonstrated the use of masking to filter.
